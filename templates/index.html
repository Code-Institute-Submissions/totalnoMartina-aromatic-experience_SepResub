How This All Started

Welcome and thank you, dear reader, for being here with me on this interesting journey covered in herbs, flowers and plant-power. I have been working with Doterra Company with distributing collections of essential oils, and all this from home. Recently, I came to realize that my friends enjoy the blends I come up with and have been encouraging me to share my insights on this topic. As I am also a writer, I decided to take up on this challenge and create a community platform for all of us passionate about blends and essential oils. I hope we all put together a nice collection of blended oils for any occasion. If you have some ideas and would like to contribute to this platform, feel free to leave a comment relating to this, and I am happy to have a video call.

Oils origin

A variety of plants cover the Earth in different shapes, sizes, colors, and scents. Many of these plants contain compounds that typically can't be seen with the naked eyeâ€”they hide within the roots, flowers, seeds, bark, or other areas of the plant. These compounds are known as essential oils. They are highly concentrated, making them extremely potent. Also referred to as volatile aromatic compounds, essential oils give a plant its aroma, protect it from harsh environmental conditions and insects, and even play a part in plant pollination.
